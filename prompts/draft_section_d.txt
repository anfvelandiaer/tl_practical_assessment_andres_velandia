TECHNICAL DECISION RECORD (BASE)

CONTEXT

- Project: Multi-country Digital Direct Channel.
- Objective: Define governance and communication patterns for high availability and scalability.  


DECISION 1: GOVERNANCE MODEL

- Requirement: Compare centralized platform vs. decentralized team-owned integrations.
- Options
    1. Centralized.
    2. Federated/Decentralized
- Decision: Federated Team-Owned Integrations.
- Reasoning: Local teams need autonomy to ensure rapid time-to-market in multiple countries, supported by a Center for Enablement (C4E) to provide reusable assets.

DECISION 2: COMMUNICATION PATTERNS

- Requirement: Compare event-driven vs. synchronous request-response for critical flows.
- Options:
    1. Synchronous.
    2.Event-Driven Architecture.
- Decision: Event-Driven Architecture (EDA) for critical persistence flows.
- Reasoning: Decouples the frontend from Salesforce to guarantee transaction delivery even during core downtime, improving resilience in Insurtech operations.

CONSEQUENCES (PROS & CONS)
Pros (Consequences):
- High resilience and availability for mission-critical processes. 
- Faster local delivery cycles through team autonomy. 
- Independent scaling of regional workloads.  

Cons (Risks):
- Increased complexity in distributed tracing.  
- Requires transition to an eventual consistency model.  
- Necessity for advanced observability (OpenTelemetry).
